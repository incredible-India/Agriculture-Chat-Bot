Farmer Govt. Schemes
Schemes for Indian Farmers

PM-KISAN

Provides ₹6,000 per year in 3 equal installments to all landholding farmers for income support.

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana

Affordable crop insurance to protect farmers against crop loss due to natural calamities and pests.

Soil Health Card Scheme

Promotes balanced use of fertilizers based on soil testing for better productivity and sustainability.

e-NAM (National Agriculture Market)

An online trading platform to connect mandis across India, helping farmers get better prices.

Kisan Credit Card (KCC)

Ensures timely credit to farmers for agricultural needs, including seeds, fertilizers, and harvesting.

AgriStack (Digital Agri Mission)

Digital agriculture ecosystem aiming to personalize advisory and financial services to farmers.
